About Anthony

Dr. Chin Loy graduated from Howard College of Medicine and did his post graduate training at both Howard University and University of California Irvine. He is currently a full time dad to his two daughters. He stays active in Functional Medicine directed at lifestyle and diet change and recently became Human Foundations certified in Functional Patterns. He has achieved the rank of Brown Belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu
